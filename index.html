Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-13

ObjRecall (“the App”) is provided by Mehrdad S. Beni (PhD) and Gary Tse (PhD) -- XRadion Dynamics (“we/us”). This page explains how the App handles data.

Summary

  • No data collection: The App does not collect, transmit, store, sell, or share personal data.
  • No internet services: The App does not send any content or telemetry to external servers.
  • No third-party SDKs: The App uses only standard Android components.
  • No permissions: The App does not request runtime permissions (e.g., Camera, Microphone, Location, Storage).

Data Handling

All inputs (e.g., prescreen answers, on-screen drawings, gameplay interactions) are processed on device only to provide features such as showing results during the session. The App does not create user accounts and does not persist your inputs to external services.

Advertising & Analytics

The App contains no advertising and no analytics.

Device Permissions

The App does not request any Android permissions. If a future version needs a permission for a new feature, this policy will be updated and the permission will be used solely for its stated purpose.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no server-side storage. Standard operating-system safeguards apply to any temporary, on-device processing.

Changes to This Policy

If we change features or data practices, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date.

Contact
